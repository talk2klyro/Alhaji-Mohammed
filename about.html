About Us

AM&S FARM is based in Damaturu, Yobe State. We are a modern agricultural enterprise that merges traditional knowledge with cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to deliver healthy, organic produce while uplifting our community.

Mission: To grow nutritious food using eco-friendly practices.
Vision: To be a leading example of sustainable farming across West Africa.

2010: Began as a small family farm with millet and sorghum.
2015: Expanded into poultry, goats, and dairy cattle.
2018: Introduced organic fertilizer trials and crop rotation.
2020: Adopted drone monitoring and smart irrigation.
2023: Launched community workshops for young farmers.
