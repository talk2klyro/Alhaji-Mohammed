About the Farmer

Alhaji Mohammed Aliyu Sulaiman is a modern farmer in Damaturu, Yobe State, who blends traditional farming methods with modern technology. He produces organic crops and healthy livestock while educating the community about sustainable agriculture.

2010: Started small-scale crop farming with millet and sorghum.
2015: Introduced livestock: goats, chickens, and cows.
2020: Adopted smart irrigation and drone-based soil monitoring.
2023: Recognized as a pioneer of sustainable innovation in northern Nigeria.
